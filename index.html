Color → PNG → data-URI

What's this?

Converts a color into a 1x1 pixel PNG represented by a data-URI. That's it.

What for?

Because IE7 and IE8 don't support rgba() backgrounds, but they do support PNG and data-URI's.

How to work this tool:

Just type or paste in any valid color, and it'll be converted into a PNG. No need to press the convert button.
